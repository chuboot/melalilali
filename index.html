About Us

Explore every corner of
Bali With Us

Melalilali.com is a travel agency based in Bali, Indonesia. We are the only tour and travel agency that offers a one-stop-shop for all your needs when you come to Bali.


Our mission is to make sure our customers have an unforgettable experience during their stay in Bali. We want to make sure that you get to know Bali's culture and nature, which is why we put so much effort into creating packages that are unique, inclusive, and easy to access.


We offer tours and transportation services that can be booked digitally or over the phone—all while still giving you the personal touch of a local guide who knows the ins and outs of the island!
